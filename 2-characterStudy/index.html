Here's a timeline of his life

  • 1869 - Born in Gujarat India
  • 1883 - Marries Kasturbai Makhanji
  • 1888 - Travels to London to learn law
  • 1891 - Returns to India after passing the bar
  • 1893 - Travels to South Africa for work
  • 1893 - Is ejected from a train motivating him to get involved in activism for rights
  • 1913 - Oraganizes "Great March" for Indian rights in South Africa
  • 1917 - Goes to Champaran to investigte condition of local farmers
  • 1917 - Returns to India after passing the bar
  • 1917 - Establishes Ashram
  • 1920 - Begins non cooperation movement
  • 1930 - Salt march to the sea
  • 1944 - Kasturba Gandhi passes away at age 74
  • 1946 - Formation of interim government of India
  • 1947 - UK passes Indian independence act
  • 1948 - Gandhi is assasinated
